Kaashi Foundation
Food & Community All ages
Duties
Help out wherever needed. Open Tues, Wed, Fri, Sat from 4:30–6:30 PM. Arrive 15 min early to help set up.
Qualifications
None required. Open to all volunteers willing to help.
Visit kaashifoundation.org
Simi Valley Youth Council
Youth & Government Grades 7–12

The Simi Valley Youth Council is an advisory board to the City Council on issues affecting youth in the community. Composed of 21 members and up to three alternates, representing every high school and middle school in Simi Valley, as well as the Community at Large.

Qualifications
Must be in 7th–12th grade in the next school year.
Visit simivalley.org
Teen Advisory Group
Youth & Government Grades 7–12
Duties
Members help shape the library's teen program by planning events, selecting books and movies for the collection, creating displays, decorating the Teen Lounge, and reviewing books.
Qualifications
Open to teens entering the 7th through 12th grades who are interested in making their voices heard. Application can be found on their website.
Visit simivalleylibrary.org/teen-advisory-group
BRITE Youth
Advocacy Youth (Generally ages 12 through 25)
Duties
Attend youth coalition meetings, participate in research projects, and collaborate with local government to address community concerns. Create and perform interactive workshops to address community issues like underage drinking and impaired driving.
Qualifications
Open to teens entering the 7th through 12th grades who are interested in making their voices heard. Application can be found on their website.
Visit briteyouth.org/bfi
California Association of Student Councils
Youth & Government High School Students
Duties
Youth Advocacy: Act as a local and statewide voice for student views and lobby for youth-oriented legislation in Sacramento California Association of Student Councils.Per Training: Lead and facilitate workshops, retreats, and training conferences teaching skills like conflict resolution, meeting management, and project planning California Association of Student Councils. Proposal: Participate in statewide conferences to create and present formal proposals to the California State Board of Education or state legislative committees California Association of Student Councils.
Qualifications
Open to high school students who are interested in student government, leadership, and advocacy. Must be willing to commit time to attend meetings, conferences, and events.State council and region cabinet positions usually require an application and a selection/interview process
Visit casc.org

Simi Valley Historical Society
History & Culture Ages 12+
Duties
Provide tours on Simi Valley history, help preserve historic items at Strathern Historical Park, or assist in the gift shop. Mon–Fri 9 AM–3 PM, Sat–Sun 1–4 PM.
Qualifications
Junior Docent Program: Must be at least 12 years old and interested in the history of Simi Valley.
Visit simihistory.com
Simi Valley Public Library
Education Ages 14+
Duties
straightening books, prepping craft supplies, or working on special projects and assisting with events and programs as needed.
Qualifications
14+, friendly, have a high level of attention to detail and can work with minimal supervision.
Visit simivalleypubliclibrary.org
Simi Valley Youth Employment Service
Education Ages 16+
Duties
Capable of committing to a two-year term, attending monthly meetings (typically on the third Wednesday at 8:15 a.m. at City Hall). Advise the City Council on the specific expectations, needs, and challenges facing local youth in the job market.
Qualifications
16+, reside in Simi Valley
Visit simivalleypubliclibrary.org

Duties

Simi Valley Police Explorers
Law Enforcement 14+ / 7th grade
Duties
Trained in law enforcement areas, assist police at community events, and learn evidence collection and report writing.
Qualifications
Must attend weekly meetings every Monday at 5:00 PM. Must be at least 14 years old or in 7th grade. Application required.
Visit svpdexplorers.com

Simi Valley Youth Orchestra (SVYO)
Arts & Music Under 19

SVYO is all about helping young musicians grow — both as artists and as people. Inspiring a love of music, building lasting skills, and creating a space where discipline meets the joy of making music. An incredible journey of musical discovery, growth, and excellence.

Qualifications
Must be 19 years old or younger and successfully audition for a position in the orchestra.
Visit svyo.net
