Volunteer Descriptions

Everything you need to know before committing to a volunteer role — duties, qualifications, hours, and direct links to official websites.

Disclaimer: Only the main duties and qualifications are listed. Always check the organization's official website before volunteering. Hope you find a great fit!

Sewa International
Food & Community All ages
Duties
Help out wherever needed. Open Tues, Wed, Fri, Sat from 4:30–6:30 PM. Arrive 15 min early to help set up.
Qualifications
None required. Open to all volunteers willing to help.
Visit sewausa.org
Simi Valley Youth Council
Youth & Government Grades 7–12

The Simi Valley Youth Council is an advisory board to the City Council on issues affecting youth in the community. Composed of 21 members and up to three alternates, representing every high school and middle school in Simi Valley, as well as the Community at Large.

Qualifications
Must be in 7th–12th grade in the next school year.
Visit simivalley.org
Simi at the Garden
Environment All ages
Duties
Help out at monthly workdays and garden projects. Visit the website to register your interest in volunteering.
Qualifications
None required.
Visit simiatthegarden.org
Simi Valley Historical Society
History & Culture Ages 12+
Duties
Provide tours on Simi Valley history, help preserve historic items at Strathern Historical Park, or assist in the gift shop. Mon–Fri 9 AM–3 PM, Sat–Sun 1–4 PM.
Qualifications
Junior Docent Program: Must be at least 12 years old and interested in the history of Simi Valley.
Visit simihistory.com
Rancho Simi Recreation & Park District
Recreation Ages 12+
Duties
Assist with special events, office tasks, recreation classes, hiking trails, and preschool/afternoon programs.
Qualifications
Must be at least 12 years old and complete an orientation available on the website.
Visit rsrpd.org
Simi Valley Police Explorers
Law Enforcement 14+ / 7th grade
Duties
Trained in law enforcement areas, assist police at community events, and learn evidence collection and report writing.
Qualifications
Must attend weekly meetings every Monday at 5:00 PM. Must be at least 14 years old or in 7th grade. Application required.
Visit svpdexplorers.com
Habitat for Humanity of Ventura County
Community Ages 12+
Duties
Help at the ReStore, serve as a photographer, or assist at special events. Sign-up required in advance.
Qualifications
Ages 12–14 may volunteer with a parent/guardian. Must be 14+ to volunteer at ReStore.
Visit habitatventura.org
The Salvation Army Simi Valley
Food & Relief All ages
Duties
Prepare food bags during pantry hours for community members in need.
Qualifications
None required. Just be ready to help wherever needed.
Visit salvationarmy.org
Ventura County Animal Services
Animals Ages 13+
Duties
Animal care, fundraising, and helping staff with various tasks at the county shelter.
Qualifications
Minimum age 13. Parent/guardian permission required. Must follow training protocols.
Visit vcas.us

Education Grade 6+

Food w/ parent/guardian
Duties

Qualifications

Simi Valley Youth Orchestra (SVYO)
Arts & Music Under 19

SVYO is all about helping young musicians grow — both as artists and as people. Inspiring a love of music, building lasting skills, and creating a space where discipline meets the joy of making music. An incredible journey of musical discovery, growth, and excellence.

Qualifications
Must be 19 years old or younger and successfully audition for a position in the orchestra.
Visit svyo.net
