Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about signing up, remote options, and group volunteering.
How do I sign up to volunteer?

Open a listing and follow the "Details" link to the organization's signup page. Some groups accept walk-up volunteers — check the listing for instructions.

Are there remote volunteering opportunities?

Yes. Search for "remote" or "virtual" in the search box to surface roles you can do from home, such as mentoring and admin support.

Can my group or team volunteer together?

Many organizations schedule group shifts. Contact the organization directly via their website to request a group volunteer slot.

How do I find opportunities in nearby cities like Thousand Oaks?

Include nearby city names in the search box (for example "Thousand Oaks" or "Moorpark") to find opportunities in neighboring communities.
