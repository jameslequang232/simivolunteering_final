Our Mission

Volunteering made simple

Simi Volunteering aims to make volunteering and helping others easy and straightforward — in the hope that you can make a real difference in your community.

Whether you're a student looking for service hours, a retiree with time to spare, or a business that wants to connect with volunteers — this is the place for you.

How It Works

Three easy steps to start giving back

1
Browse Opportunities

Explore our curated catalog of volunteer opportunities in Simi Valley. Filter by organization type, age requirement, or schedule.

2
Read the Details

Each listing includes duties, qualifications, hours, and a direct link to the organization's website so you can get all the facts first.

3
Show Up & Volunteer

Contact the organization directly and show up ready to help. Every hour you give strengthens the Simi Valley community.
